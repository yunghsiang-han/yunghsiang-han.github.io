課程名稱：現代編碼技術 (Modern Coding Technology)

授課老師：韓永祥

課程目標：本課程之目標是教導學生線性碼之軟性決定解碼知識。

成績考查方式：期中專題40％，期末專題40％，平常成績佔20％

A set of lecture notes will be provided on my personal website. You may obtain the newest version of the lecture notes from http://ct.ee.ntust.edu.tw/welcome.html. The following is a list of recommended references:

1. Channel Coding for Telecommunications, Martin Bossert, John Wiley & Sons, 1999.

2. Turbo Codes — Principles and Applications, B. Vucetic and J. Yuan, Kluwer Academic Publishers, 2000.

3. Error-Control Coding for Data Networks, I. S. Reed and X. Chen, Kluwer Academic Publishers, 1999.

 

Students’final grades for this course are based on their performances in midterm project reports , final project reports on problems in the coding theory research area, and up to 120-minute oral presentations on their reports. Students will be asked to attend every lecture in order to learn the advance topics of decoding.

課程綱要：

1.        Soft-decision decoding for linear block codes

2.        Introduction to convolutional codes

3.        Sequential decoding of binary convolutional codes

4.        Turbo decoding

5.        Low density parity-check codes
