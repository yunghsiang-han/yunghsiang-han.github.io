課程名稱：數位邏輯 (Digital Logic)

授課老師：韓永祥

課程目標：本課程之目標是教導學生數位邏輯知識。

成績考查方式：小考60％，期末考試30％，平常成績佔10％

 

A set of lecture notes will be provided on blackboard. You may obtain the newest version of the lecture notes from blackboard.  Students will be asked to attend every lecture in order to learn the material.

 

The following is the textbook:

Digital Design, Fourth Ed., M. M. Mano and M. D. Ciletti, Person Edition, 2007.

 

課程綱要：

1.     Digital Systems and Binary Numbers

2.     Boolean algebra and Logic Gates

3.     Gate-Level Minimization

4.     4. Combinational Logic

5.     Synchronous Sequential Logic

6.     Registers and Counters

7.     Memory and Programmable Logic
